Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

A full-featured music player with a clean interface, built for people who just want to listen.

Millions of Songs

Search and stream from a massive music library with no ads and no subscriptions required.

Synced Lyrics

Follow along with real-time synced lyrics that scroll with the music as you listen.

Smart Queue

When your queue runs out, Snowify keeps the music going by automatically adding similar songs.

Playlists

Organize your music with fully customizable playlists, complete with drag and drop support.

Artist Pages

Dedicated artist pages with top songs, discography, audience stats, and similar artists.

Discord Rich Presence

Show what you're currently listening to right on your Discord profile.

Cloud Sync

Your library, playlists, and liked songs stay in sync across all your devices automatically.

Spotify Import

Easily import your existing Spotify playlists into Snowify with no extra setup needed.

Themes

Multiple built-in color themes so you can personalize the look to match your style.

Music Videos

Watch music videos directly in the app without ever leaving your playlist.

Follow Artists

Follow your favorite artists and see their new releases right on your home page.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Full keyboard control for playback, seeking, volume, and search so you never need the mouse.
