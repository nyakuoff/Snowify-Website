Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

A full-featured music player with a clean interface, built for people who just want to listen.

Millions of Songs

Search and stream from a massive music library with no ads and no subscriptions required.

Synced Lyrics

Real-time lyrics that scroll with the music, sourced from Musixmatch, LrcLib, and Netease.

Spotify Import

Bring your existing playlists over from Spotify instantly.

Cloud Sync

Your library, playlists, and liked songs stay in sync across all your devices automatically.

Playlists — Create, organize, and drag-and-drop your music
Artist Pages — Top songs, discography, audience stats, and similar artists
Discord RPC — Show what you're listening to on your profile
Smart Queue — Keeps the music going by automatically adding similar songs
Themes — Multiple built-in color themes to match your style
Music Videos — Watch videos directly without leaving the app
Follow Artists — Get new releases on your home page
Keyboard Shortcuts — Full keyboard control for everything
